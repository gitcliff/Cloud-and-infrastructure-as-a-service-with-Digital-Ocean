## How to Create a Free Tier Droplet on DigitalOcean

Follow these detailed steps to create a free (or trial) tier droplet on DigitalOcean:

### 1. Sign Up for a DigitalOcean Account
1. Go to [https://www.digitalocean.com/](https://www.digitalocean.com/).
2. Click **Sign Up** and register using your email, Google, or GitHub account.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Add a valid payment method (credit card or PayPal). DigitalOcean often provides a free trial credit for new users.

### 2. Access the DigitalOcean Control Panel
1. Log in to your DigitalOcean account.
2. You will be redirected to the [DigitalOcean Control Panel](https://cloud.digitalocean.com/login).

### 3. Create a New Droplet
1. Click the **Create** button in the top right corner and select **Droplets**.
2. Choose an operating system (e.g., Ubuntu 22.04 LTS is recommended for most users).

### 4. Select a Plan
1. Under **Choose a plan**, select the **Basic** plan.
2. Pick the lowest-cost option (usually 1 vCPU, 512MB or 1GB RAM). This is covered by the free trial credit.

### 5. Choose a Data Center Region
1. Select a region closest to your users or yourself for best performance.

### 6. Authentication
1. Choose **Password** or **SSH Key** authentication.
	- For password: Enter a strong password.
	- For SSH key: Add your public SSH key (recommended for security).
2. We will use SSH key for best practices and security     

### 7. Final Configuration
1. (Optional) Add backups, monitoring, or additional options as needed.
2. Enter a hostname for your droplet (e.g., `my-first-droplet`).

### 8. Create the Droplet
1. Click **Create Droplet**.
2. Wait a few moments for the droplet to be provisioned.

### 9. Access Your Droplet
1. Once created, you will see your droplet's IP address in the dashboard.
2. Connect via SSH:
	- If you chose password: `ssh root@<your_droplet_ip>` and enter your password.
	- If you used an SSH key: `ssh root@<your_droplet_ip>` (no password needed).

### 10. Manage and Monitor
1. Use the DigitalOcean dashboard to monitor resource usage, reboot, or destroy your droplet as needed.
2. Remember, the free trial is credit-based. Monitor your usage to avoid charges after the credit expires.

---
**Note:** DigitalOcean does not have a permanent free tier, but new users receive free credits to try the platform. Always check the latest offers and terms on the DigitalOcean website.
